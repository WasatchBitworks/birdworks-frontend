About This Data

This page provides detailed visualizations of bird detection data collected by our BirdNET-Pi system. All timestamps are displayed in Mountain Time (MT), and detection confidence levels are calculated by the AI model.

Hourly Activity

Shows when birds are most active throughout the day. Dawn and dusk typically show peak activity.

Weekly Patterns

Reveals detection patterns across different days of the week, helping identify behavioral trends.

Extended Timeline

Track detection trends over weeks and months to observe seasonal changes and migration patterns.

Species Comparison

Compare detection frequencies across different species to understand which birds are most common in our area.
