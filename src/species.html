About Our Species Data

Our BirdNET-Pi system uses the BirdNET 6K Global Model, which can identify over 6,000 bird species worldwide. The species shown here are those that have been detected in our monitoring area on the Wasatch Front, Utah.

Detection counts include all confidence levels. Higher detection counts generally indicate more common species in the area.
